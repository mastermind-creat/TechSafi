FOR STUDENTS

Internship & Industrial Attachment

Launch your tech career with TechSafi! We offer comprehensive internship and industrial attachment programs designed to give students real-world experience in cutting-edge technology projects.

Hands-On Experience

Work on real projects alongside experienced developers. Gain practical skills in web development, mobile apps, AI solutions, and cloud technologies.

Mentorship Program

Get paired with experienced mentors who will guide you through your learning journey and help you develop both technical and professional skills.

Career Development

Receive certificates, recommendation letters, and potential job offers for outstanding performers. Build your portfolio with real-world projects.

Internship Program

3-6 Months Duration

What You'll Get:

  • Monthly stipend for your contributions
  • Flexible working hours (part-time/full-time)
  • Remote or hybrid work options
  • Access to learning resources and tools
  • Certificate of completion
  • Potential for full-time employment

Requirements: Currently enrolled in university/college pursuing IT, Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.

Industrial Attachment

2-4 Months Duration

What You'll Get:

  • Structured training program
  • Dedicated supervisor and mentor
  • Weekly progress reviews and feedback
  • Official attachment letter for your institution
  • Final assessment report and recommendation
  • Portfolio-worthy project experience

Requirements: Students required to complete industrial attachment as part of their academic program.
