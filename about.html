Our Story

Founded in 2024, TechSafi was born out of resilience and ambition. After finishing college and facing the tough reality of unemployment, we decided to turn our skills into solutions. Building a software company that could create opportunities not just for us, but for others too.

What started as a small group of passionate tech enthusiasts has evolved into a full-fledged technology company providing innovative digital solutions for individuals, businesses, and enterprises. From custom web applications to intelligent automation systems, we focus on delivering clean, scalable, and impactful technology that drives real results.

Our journey is guided by innovation, integrity, and a deep belief that technology can empower growth. Today, TechSafi continues to help clients transform their operations, enhance their digital presence, and unlock their full potential in an ever-evolving digital world.

Our Journey

A timeline of our growth, achievements, and milestones over the years

2015 - Foundation

TechSafi was founded with a small team of 5 people working out of a shared office space. Our first client project was a web application for a local startup.

2017 - First Major Milestone

Reached 25 employees and moved to our first dedicated office. Launched our mobile app development service, which quickly became one of our most popular offerings.

2019 - Recognition

Named "Fastest Growing Tech Company" by Tech Magazine. Expanded our services to include AI and machine learning solutions, opening up new opportunities in enterprise markets.

2021 - Global Expansion

Opened our first international office in London, UK. Our team grew to over 100 employees across three continents, serving clients in 15+ countries.

2023 - Today

Recognized as a leader in digital transformation services. Continuing to innovate and expand our service offerings while maintaining our commitment to excellence and client success.
